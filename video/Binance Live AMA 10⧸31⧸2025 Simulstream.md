## Technical Claims Analysis

### What BlockDAG Claims to Offer

From the AMA transcript, BlockDAG makes the following technical assertions:

#### 1. **Hybrid UTXO + EVM Architecture**
> "Block lag introduces a new approach to blockchain scalability by combining the standard of two core model in the kit world, the UTX model known for the speed and privacy and account best model known for enabling smart contact and decentralized application." (Lines 3-6)

**Claim:** Combines Bitcoin/Kaspa-style UTXO with Ethereum's EVM for smart contracts.

#### 2. **GhostDAG Protocol**
> "powered by co-stag, a protocol, allowing multiple block to created and validated in parallel" (Lines 6-7)

**Claim:** Uses a DAG consensus mechanism for parallel block processing.

#### 3. **EVM Compatibility**
> "block lag is also fully EVM compatible, meaning the developers can deploy and migrate their exciting existing ethereum, depth, token and smart contacts with minimal adjustments" (Lines 8-10)

**Claim:** Full Ethereum compatibility for easy dApp migration.
