## üéØ In Plain English (Layman's Terms)

### What's a TGE?

**TGE** = Token Generation Event = Launch Day = The day your tokens become real and tradeable.

**40% TGE** means on launch day, you get access to 40% of your tokens. The other 60% stays locked and releases over time.

### The Promise vs The Math

**The Promise**: "You get 40% of your tokens at launch!"

**The Math Problem**: 
```
We have $32 million in the bank to back the token.
If we release 40% of tokens (6.8 billion), each token is worth:

$32,000,000 √∑ 6,800,000,000 = $0.0047 per token

That's less than HALF A PENNY.
```

### Think of it Like a Pizza

Imagine you have one pizza ($32M) and you need to share it with 6.8 billion people (tokens).

- **With 40% TGE**: Each person gets a tiny crumb ($0.0047)
- **With 2% TGE**: The pizza is only shared with 340 million people, so each gets a bigger slice ($0.094)

**Same pizza. Different number of people sharing it.**

### What "Emergency Brake" Means

When price drops too low ($0.002) or liquidity drops too low ($10M), the system **freezes** further token releases to stop the bleeding.

- **Early brake** (Month 1-3): Very bad - crash happened immediately
- **Mid brake** (Month 4-12): Challenging but manageable
- **Late brake** (Month 13+): System working well
- **No brake**: Best case - market is stable enough
