## Conclusion

The emergency brake system activates in **100% of tested scenarios**, demonstrating it works as designed. The key variables are:

| Factor | Impact on Outcome |
|--------|-------------------|
| **Liquidity stability** | PRIMARY - determines survivability |
| **Time before first crash** | More time = more investor access |
| **Recovery possibility** | Extends runway significantly |
| **Emission controls** | 20% cap improves all scenarios |
| **Staking rate** | Higher = better price stability |

**Bottom Line**: At $32M liquidity, no scenario maintains $0.05 price long-term. The emergency brake's purpose is to **protect remaining value** when conditions deteriorate, not to prevent natural market price discovery.
